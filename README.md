# 🚖 City Fare Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

The City Fare Analysis dashboard is a comprehensive data analytics project that visualizes urban transportation metrics for the year 2024. It provides a detailed breakdown of **148.76K total bookings**, monitoring everything from revenue streams and payment preferences to cancellation reasons and vehicle performance.

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- **Total Bookings:** 148,767 total ride requests processed.
- **Success Rate:** 61.97% of bookings were successfully completed.
- **Average Fare:** Maintained at approximately $3.50 per trip.
- **Cancellation Rate:** An overall cancellation percentage of 25%.

---

## 🚀 Core Insights

- **Payment Preferences:** UPI is the leading payment method with a total booking value of $20.3M, followed by Cash at $11.1M.
- **Vehicle Performance:** Uber XL generated the highest total distance traveled at 13M km, while Auto rides accounted for the highest individual booking count among segments.
- **Cancellation Analysis:** Rides are analyzed by both Customer and Driver cancellation reasons, including "Change of plans," "Driver asked to cancel," and "AC not working".
- **Distance Categories:** Revenue is segmented into Short (0-5km), Medium (5-15km), Long (15-30km), and Extra Long (30+ km) to identify the most profitable trip types.
- **Time-Series Trends:** Tracks booking fluctuations and revenue across months (January to December) and day parts (Morning, Afternoon, Evening, Night).

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- **Power BI:** For interactive data visualization and dashboarding.
- **Data Analysis:** Time-series forecasting, revenue tiering, and service quality scoring.

---

## 📂 Project Structure

- **Overview Dashboard:** High-level KPIs and fare trends.
- **Booking Summary:** Detailed breakdown of success and failure rates.
- **Vehicle Analysis:** Deep dive into vehicle-specific distances and ratings.
- **Revenue Analysis:** Breakdown by payment method and distance category.
